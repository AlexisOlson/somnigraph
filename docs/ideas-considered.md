# Ideas Considered — carsteneu ai-memory-comparison deep survey

*Consolidated 2026-06-30. Companion to [`declined.md`](declined.md) (the negative space), the curated system profiles in [`similar-systems.md`](similar-systems.md), and the 59 new files in [`research/sources/`](../research/sources/).*

This is the **positive** half of the carsteneu directory survey: every concrete mechanism worth knowing about that surfaced when we read all 59 previously-untouched systems at the code level (not README triage). It is a **ledger of ideas, not a backlog** — an idea appearing here means "we saw it, it is real, here is where it would land in Somnigraph and roughly what it would cost." Adoption is a separate decision made against the priority list in `STEWARDSHIP.md`.

Directive that governed the pass (Alexis): **err on the side of wasting tokens rather than losing a valuable idea.** So the bar for logging a nugget is low; the bar for calling it *adopt* is high.

## Provenance & headline

- **Source**: the [carsteneu/ai-memory-comparison](https://github.com/carsteneu/ai-memory-comparison) directory. 15 of its rows already had full analyses; the remaining **59** (50 cold + 9 previously nugget-mined) were surveyed here, one Opus agent per system: `git clone --depth 1`, read the retrieval / write-path / consolidation / decay code, cross-check the system's `evidence/<name>.md` against the code, write a template file, return a structured nugget list.
- **Result**: **13 DIVE / 41 MAYBE / 5 SKIP**, **137 nuggets** (17 *adopt*, 87 *consider*, 33 *note-only*), 124/137 additive. This is a sharp correction over the earlier light-touch triage (0 DIVE / 7 MAYBE / 50 SKIP) — **the deep read is what found the ideas.** mcp-memory-service was nearly a light-touch SKIP and holds an adopt-tier benchmark harness; token-savior was SKIP'd and ships a working contextual-bandit injection ranker.
- Each system's full analysis is at `research/sources/<file>.md`; the per-system verdict + one-line compare + evidence cross-check is tabulated in [`declined.md` Part 1](declined.md#part-1--comparison-table-rows-carsteneu-directory-surveyed-at-code-level).

## How to read the tiers

- **adopt** — concrete, code-grounded, additive, and pointed at a real Somnigraph gap. A short-list for `STEWARDSHIP.md` to rank, not a commitment.
- **consider** — real and additive, but either narrower, higher-effort, or overlapping something we half-have. The bulk of the value beyond the headline.
- **note-only** — logged for completeness: redundant with what we have, out of scope, or a design-tension note where we deliberately chose the other way. Kept so a future session does not re-discover it.
- Every row names the module it would touch and an effort estimate. "additive vs redundant" is the agent's call on whether it adds a capability or restates one we have.

## Recurring themes (the real signal)

The single most valuable output of the survey is not any one nugget but the **convergence**: dozens of independent systems keep arriving at the same handful of moves, and they cluster tightly around the gaps Somnigraph already names in its own "What Somnigraph lacks" list. Independent convergence is strong evidence.

1. **Move reconciliation to the write path** — the biggest cluster by far. Give `remember()` a cheap synchronous dedup/update/supersede gate instead of deferring *all* typed-edge and merge work to sleep. Seen as a 4-way AUDN/ADD-UPDATE-NOOP-DELETE cascade in **Memory Palace** (Write Guard), **AIPass**, **mem9**, **memoir**, **second-brain**, **TencentDB-AM** (batched), **Honcho** (dedup+reinforce), **memory-lancedb-pro** (typed relationship classification), **engram** (topic_key upsert), **omega-memory** (supersession heuristic + canonical-hash). This is the direct answer to our named "no real-time graph construction / no write-path quality gating" gaps — sleep stays the source of truth, but a pre-sleep floor stops near-duplicates from ever landing.

2. **Write-path quality / salience / noise gating** — corroborates the Phase-18 finding that **write quality, not retrieval, is the LoCoMo lever**. Cheap gates before an expensive write: **m_flow** (`worth.py` full/summary/none), **MemLayer** (prototype-margin), **VIR** (confidence funnel), **opencode-mem** / **Nanobot** (LLM salience skip + rubric), **Noosphere** (`validateDurableContent`), **Kage** (pending inbox), **memory-lancedb-pro** (grounding-F1 gate + self-growing noise bank), **mcp-memory-service** (composite quality score).

3. **Secret/PII redaction at ingest** — **Memora** (~13 regex patterns → `[REDACTED]`), **Noosphere** (`SECRET_PATTERNS`), **claude-mem** (`<private>`-tag strip). Cheap, low-risk, and a real hole: nothing stops Somnigraph from embedding a leaked key today.

4. **Attack the multi-hop vocabulary gap from both ends** — the ~88% ceiling our own `benchmarks.md` § Multi-hop vocabulary gap names. **Write side**: atomic-fact decomposition + MaxSim eviction (**EverOS**, **Octopoda-OS**), anticipated-queries (**YesMem**), entity-bridge edges (**YourMemory**, +12pp in their ablation). **Query side**: min-cost evidence-path scoring (**m_flow**), HyDE + decomposition (**ArcRift**, **omega-memory**), offline vocab_map (**fidelis**), coreference resolution (**m_flow**).

5. **Consume the contradiction signal at recall time** — we *detect* contradiction/evolves edges in sleep and then **ignore them at live recall**. Multiple systems close that loop: hard trust-tier bucket so a disputed memory can't outrank an undisputed one (**icarus**), contradiction-survival counter as a durability feature (**second-brain**), currency surfaced not filtered (**Midas**), memory-carried counter-arguments (**gitmem**), `as_of` time-travel (**Memvid**, **memanto**, **Wax**).

6. **Faithfulness / hallucination guards on generated prose** — cheap lexical gates on sleep-generated summaries/gestalts: overlap floor (**Origin**), anti-contamination entity scan (**ClawMem**), integer-ID indirection so the merge LLM can't invent IDs (**mem9**), provenance-hash staleness on derived memories (**Memory Palace**, **icarus**, **Kage**).

7. **Eval honesty** — directly serves STEWARDSHIP Priority 4: a deterministic dumb-reader ablation floor (**Midas**) and a reproducible **zero-LLM LongMemEval-S retrieval harness** (**mcp-memory-service**) that fills our total lack of external LME numbers.

8. **Measure before you threshold** — independently matches our own honest-accounting ethos: shadow-mode NN-cosine logging before setting a dedup cutoff (**mem9**), mid-similarity sweet-spot band for consolidation pairs (**mcp-memory-service**), simulate-before-mutate forgetting (**Memory Palace**).

9. **Inputs to the stalled proactive-injection design** (`docs/proposals/proactive-injection.md`) — **token-savior** ships a live LinUCB contextual-bandit injection ranker with a delayed implicit-reward window (a feedback-free complement to our Thompson gating); **AIPass** and **obsidian-mind** ship the exact hook shape (threshold + cooldown + anti-repetition); **icarus** adds a *negative* "do-not-reuse" channel; **fidelis** a confident-abstain floor.

10. **Decay variants worth an experiment** — hybrid exp→power-law tail avoiding the cliff (**shodh-memory**, Wixted-cited), ACT-R spacing activation (**MemoryBear** — and note: its ACT-R decay *is* implemented, correcting our prior "unrealized" note), spaced-repetition accumulation (**agentmemory-rohitg00**), activity-clocked decay so quiet projects don't age (**YesMem**, **YourMemory**), ROI-vs-cost eviction lens (**token-savior**).

11. **Feed rejections back into sleep** — **ai-memory (akitaonrails)** persists fingerprints of *declined* consolidation suggestions and feeds the recent set back into the next sleep prompt so the LLM stops re-proposing them. This is directly convergent with this very branch's `declined.md` premise (don't redo a settled examination) and with commit `164fcb5` (dropping malformed suggestions) — the same idea, applied to the sleep loop. **stash** adds a hypothesis lifecycle (proposed→confirmed→promoted) that automates the manual "forget question → remember answer" loop in CLAUDE.md.

## Tier 1 — Adopt candidates (17)

Ranked roughly by leverage-per-effort. Each links its source analysis. These are the short-list; promotion into the research agenda ([`roadmap.md`](roadmap.md)) and ranking against the priority list (`STEWARDSHIP.md`) happen there, not here.

### Write-path reconciliation & quality (the Phase-18 lever)

1. **Write Guard: synchronous ADD/UPDATE/NOOP/DELETE dedup gate** — Medium — `tools.py remember()`. Reconcile a new memory against nearest neighbors at write time (vector ≥0.92 NOOP / ≥0.78 UPDATE; keyword ≥0.82 / ≥0.55; optional LLM tiebreak; fail-closed NOOP). Gives `remember()` the dedup+update-in-place gate it lacks. — [memory-palace.md](../research/sources/memory-palace.md)
2. **Write-time typed relationship classification** — High — `tools.py` + reuse `sleep_nrem.py` classifier inline. Classify new-vs-nearest as support/contextualize/contradict/supersede at store time and act immediately (supersede sets `valid_until`), instead of deferring every typed edge to sleep. — [memory-lancedb-pro.md](../research/sources/memory-lancedb-pro.md)
3. **Grounding-based confidence gate on write** — Low — `tools.py` write path. Cheap non-LLM ROUGE-like F1 / token-coverage of the candidate against the actual conversation spans; penalize unsupported ratio as a hallucination guard before persisting. — [memory-lancedb-pro.md](../research/sources/memory-lancedb-pro.md)
4. **Write-time secret/PII redaction** — Low — `tools.py impl_remember`. ~13 regex patterns rewrite API keys / tokens / private keys / cards to `[REDACTED]` before storage. — [memora.md](../research/sources/memora.md)
5. **Write-time dedup + reinforcement counter** — Medium — `tools.py remember()` + `db.py` + `reranker.py`. On kNN top-1 ≥0.95, keep the token-superior text, soft-delete the loser, carry a `times_derived` reinforcement counter forward — a free write-path gate *and* a cheap salience feature. — [honcho.md](../research/sources/honcho.md)

### Retrieval: attack the multi-hop vocabulary-gap ceiling

6. **Atomic-fact child decomposition + MaxSim eviction** — High — `scoring.py` (+ atomic-fact child table beside layers). Embed each atomic fact independently, re-score the parent episode by best-matching child (max-pool), let the child replace the parent when more query-relevant. Directly targets the measured multi-hop ceiling. — [everos.md](../research/sources/everos.md)
7. **Min-cost evidence-path propagation** — High — `scoring.py` PPR + `reranker.py` feature. Score a memory by the cheapest chain (`vector_dist + edge_dist + hop_penalty`) from a query hit up to it, letting queries enter at fact/entity granularity with a penalty on matching coarse summaries directly — makes the graph the scorer. — [m-flow.md](../research/sources/m-flow.md)
8. **Anticipated queries (query-to-query match)** — Medium — `embeddings.py` + `fts.py` + `reranker.py`. At write time generate 3–5 concrete search phrases the memory answers; FTS-index and embed each keyed to the parent, so differently-phrased queries hit via query-to-query similarity. — [yesmem.md](../research/sources/yesmem.md)

### Consolidation / sleep

9. **Surprisal-based consolidation targeting** — Medium — `sleep_nrem.py`. RP-tree / kNN-distance over active-memory embeddings; hand the top ~10% most novel outliers to sleep as anchors instead of recency/priority ranking. LLM-free, offline-testable against existing edges. — [honcho.md](../research/sources/honcho.md)
10. **Mid-similarity sweet-spot band for consolidation pairs** — Low — `sleep_nrem.py` candidate generation. Filter consolidation pairs to cosine 0.45–0.7, skipping near-dups and unrelated pairs, to raise non-trivial-edge yield per LLM call (our top-K biases to near-dups). — [mcp-memory-service.md](../research/sources/mcp-memory-service.md)
11. **Faithfulness gate on sleep-generated prose** — Low — `sleep_rem.py`. Require ≥50% of a generated sentence's content tokens to whole-word-match the union of sources; below the floor, ship a deterministic template instead of the LLM prose. Pure lexical, no embedding call. — [origin.md](../research/sources/origin.md)
12. **Subject-anchor guard on the destructive merge/supersede path** — Low — `tools.py` dedup + `sleep_nrem.py`. Extract proper-noun anchors from both sides; hard-reject a merge when anchor sets differ (empty or ≤50% overlap) regardless of similarity. Blocks "Dan"/"Dad" collisions at the 0.9 cutoff. Gate on a cheap offline measurement that the collision actually occurs. — [clawmem.md](../research/sources/clawmem.md)
13. **Rejection buffer for sleep suggestions** — Medium — `sleep_nrem.py` + a declined-suggestions store. Persist fingerprints of declined merge/archive/edit proposals and feed the recent-relevant set back into the next sleep prompt so the LLM stops re-proposing them. Convergent with this branch's `declined.md` and commit `164fcb5`. — [ai-memory.md](../research/sources/ai-memory.md)

### Retrieval fallback & ranking

14. **Cross-encoder reranker fallback over RRF top-k** — Medium — `scoring.py` fallback path. A feedback-free bge-reranker-v2-m3 (ONNX, CPU int8) with position-aware weighting (trust more on lower ranks, preserve top-3 exact matches), for when the learned reranker artifact is missing (currently our live state — RRF fallback only). — [omega-memory.md](../research/sources/omega-memory.md)
15. **Provenance/trust as a reranker feature** — Low — `reranker.py` (+ existing `source` label). Down-weight by source trust (user-stated > agreed > llm-extracted > web-external) at rank time. — [yesmem.md](../research/sources/yesmem.md)

### Eval honesty (STEWARDSHIP Priority 4)

16. **Port the LongMemEval-S retrieval harness** — Medium — new `scripts/benchmarks/` adapter. Zero-LLM R@5/R@10/NDCG@10/MRR by question type over the 500-Q distractor set, with an evidence-session-dedup trick so recall can't exceed 1.0. Fills our complete lack of external LME numbers. Caveat: recall-only, complements not replaces LoCoMo QA. — [mcp-memory-service.md](../research/sources/mcp-memory-service.md)
17. **Dumb-reader ablation** — Low — LoCoMo QA harness. A deterministic extractive reader (top turn by content-word overlap; verbatim=1.0 else token-F1) run beside the LLM judge, so the answer score can only move with *retrieval* quality — an eval-honesty floor against LoCoMo judge leniency. — [midas.md](../research/sources/midas.md)
